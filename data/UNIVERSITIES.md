# University directory

`us-universities.json` contains 3,930 active, degree-granting institutions in
the 50 states and Washington, D.C. from the U.S. Department of Education's
NCES/IPEDS 2024 Directory Information file (`HD2024`), retrieved on 2026-07-14.
The Check-In combobox also accepts a manually entered institution because no
third-party directory can guarantee perfect coverage.

`university-geo.json` maps every dropdown name to its IPEDS city and state.
Check-In derives region and climate locally from that location; only manually
entered institutions use the one-tap region fallback.
